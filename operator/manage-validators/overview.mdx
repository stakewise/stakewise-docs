# Manage Validators

You can self-report your validators to [Rated Network](./rated-network) for performance tracking and analytics, ensuring they appear correctly in the Rated Explorer dashboard.

If you have legacy `0x01` validators, the [`consolidate`](./consolidate-validators) command lets you merge them into `0x02` compound validators with balances up to 2048 ETH, reducing validator count and infrastructure overhead. You can target specific keys or let the Operator Service consolidate automatically.

For emergency, maintenance, or testing scenarios, the [`exit-validators`](./manually-exit-validators) command triggers manual validator exits — by specific indexes or by count. If you ever need to restore operations after key loss or infrastructure failure, the [`recover`](./recover-validators) command regenerates active validator keystores from your mnemonic.

The Operator Service also includes built-in commands to [automatically install and manage](./automated-node-setup) Reth (execution) and Lighthouse (consensus) clients — covering binary installation, syncing, and status monitoring.
