# Introduction

[Staking](/docs/stakewise-protocol/understanding-liquid-staking) puts your ETH to work securing Ethereum, and you earn rewards for it.

## Why Stake with StakeWise?

StakeWise makes staking simple, flexible, and secure. Your funds always stay in non-custodial smart contracts — stake any amount of ETH, withdraw whenever you want, and mint [osETH](/docs/ostoken/intro), a liquid token you can trade or use across DeFi. Your stake auto-compounds over time, reflected in a rising osETH:ETH exchange rate. Professional operators handle all the validator infrastructure for you, and you get to choose from multiple Vaults with different fees and performance. Built-in strategies like [Boost](/docs/vaults/boost) push your rewards even further — historically reaching 4–6% APY with a single click by using your osETH as collateral on Aave to borrow and restake more ETH. Everything runs on battle-tested, regularly audited smart contracts that secure over $1B in TVL.

## How It Works

1. You deposit ETH into a [Vault](/docs/vaults/intro) — a smart contract that pools deposits and stakes them through validators.
2. You receive osETH in return — its exchange rate against ETH rises continuously as rewards flow in.
3. You can use your osETH freely — trade it, deploy it in DeFi, or convert it back to ETH whenever you want.

That's it. No lockups, no hardware, no complexity.
