# Introduction

As you already know from the [Understanding Liquid Staking](/docs/stakewise-protocol/understanding-liquid-staking) section, liquid staking lets you earn Ethereum staking rewards without locking your assets. StakeWise makes this process simple, secure, and flexible.

With StakeWise, you can:

- **Stake without barriers** — No technical knowledge or 32 ETH minimum required.
- **Stay liquid with [osETH](/docs/ostoken/intro)** — Mint a liquid staking token that represents your staked position. Use it across DeFi without unstaking.
- **Amplify your yield with [Boost](/docs/vaults/boost)** — StakeWise's flagship strategy takes advantage of the positive spread between staking APY and the ETH borrow cost on Aave to increase your returns. Built into every Vault by default.

At the core of StakeWise is a [Vault](/docs/vaults/intro) — a smart contract that pools ETH from depositors and stakes it through validators to earn rewards. Each Vault is a completely isolated staking environment run by immutable smart contract code. StakeWise undergoes regular security audits, and Vault contracts secure over $1B in staked assets today.

## Choose Your Staking Method

- **[One-Click Staking →](./one-click-staking)** — The simplest way to stake. Connect your wallet, enter the amount, confirm. Done in seconds. Full protection from slashing and penalties.
- **[Staking with Vaults →](./staking-with-vaults)** — Choose your node operators, MEV strategy, and fee structure. Each Vault is an isolated staking environment with its own terms.
