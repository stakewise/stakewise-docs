# Rewards

Staking rewards are earned on Ethereum for proposing and verifying blocks. When you stake with StakeWise, your ETH is deposited to activate validators that perform essential network duties and earn rewards in return.

Validators on Ethereum earn three types of rewards:

- **Consensus rewards** — earned for reaching consensus on the state of Ethereum and keeping the network running.

- **Execution rewards** — priority fees paid by users to incentivize validators to prioritize their transactions.

- **MEV rewards** — additional value captured by reordering, including, or excluding transactions within a block.

All flow into the Vault where your ETH is staked, increasing the Vault's APY — the projected yearly return on your staked ETH after fees.
Your APY depends on the Vault, including the validators' performance, MEV strategy, and the Vault's fees.
