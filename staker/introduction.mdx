# Introduction

[Staking](/docs/stakewise-protocol/understanding-liquid-staking) puts your ETH to work securing Ethereum, and you earn rewards for it.

## Why Stake with StakeWise?

StakeWise makes staking simple, flexible, and secure. You stay in full control — your funds never leave the smart contracts. Stake any amount of ETH and withdraw whenever you want. Rewards auto-compound, so each share is worth more ETH over time.

Professional operators handle all the validator infrastructure for you, and you get to choose from multiple Vaults with different fees and performance. Mint [osETH](/docs/ostoken/intro), a liquid staking token you can trade or use across DeFi while your ETH keeps earning.

Built-in strategies like [Boost](/docs/vaults/boost) push your rewards even further — historically reaching 4–6% APY with a single click by using your osETH as collateral on Aave to borrow and restake more ETH. Everything runs on battle-tested, regularly audited smart contracts that secure over $1B in TVL.

## How It Works

1. You deposit ETH into a [Vault](/docs/vaults/intro) — a smart contract that pools deposits and stakes them through validators.
2. You receive osETH in return — its exchange rate against ETH rises continuously as rewards flow in.
3. You can use your osETH freely — trade it, deploy it in DeFi, or convert it back to ETH whenever you want.

That's it. No lockups, no hardware, no complexity.
